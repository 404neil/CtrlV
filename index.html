Knowledge Hub

The Knowledge Hub displays reference content (guides, SOPs, FAQs) alongside your blurbs. Content is written in Markdown. Paste image URLs or YouTube/Vimeo links on their own line to embed them.

Supports Markdown: # headings, **bold**, *italic*, [links](url), `code`. Paste a YouTube/Vimeo URL alone on a line to embed a video. Paste an image URL alone on a line to embed an image.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Set a shortcut on any blurb using the ⌨ badge next to its label. Shortcuts trigger when you press the key combo while the page is focused.

📱 On mobile? Connect a Bluetooth or USB keyboard to your device and shortcuts will work exactly the same way.
